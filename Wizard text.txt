Wizards is the tribe with the most MANA,
based on their magic skills they've developed
many potions to help them defeat their enemies.
There are three types of Wizards: Black Magic,
Grey Magic, White Magic. 

Black Magic Wizards 
Start with a little bit more damage than others,
they focus more on their damage later on.

Grey Magic Wizards
Start with a little bit more stamina and mana than 
others, they focus more on their well-being later on.

White Magic Wizards
Start with a little bit more health and mana than
others, they focus more on their health points 
later on and mana.
